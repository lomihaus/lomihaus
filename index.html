Our Story

 

The authentic taste was born out of love and respect for these humble delicious creations, met with a desire to bring quality ingredients to the table. Simply put, we're here to bring you a experience you can feel good about.

 

Lomi Haus opened on March 4, 2019. Lomihan and silogan originated in Batangas and decided to bring their delicious recipe to manila and caloocan. Now they celebrate 4 years of their classic, made with love filipino cooking.
